Privacy Policy

Pet Care Reminder (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides tools to help you manage pet care. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: email, name (when you create an account).
  • App data you add: pet profiles, reminders, notes, records.
  • Approximate location (if you allow it) to show local alerts and nearby services.
  • Device info for app performance and crash reports.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core features (reminders, records, local alerts).
  • Securely store your data and synchronize it to your devices.
  • Improve reliability and user experience.

Sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share limited data with service providers (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) to operate the app, under contracts that protect your information.

Data Storage & Security

Data is stored with reputable providers and protected by access controls. You can export or delete your data in the app at any time.

Your Choices

  • Notifications and location access are optional and can be changed in Settings.
  • Export data and delete account from Settings → Data & Privacy.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13.
